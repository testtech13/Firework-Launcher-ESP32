FWL Commands
					Command      Returns	Description
Heart Beat 			- HB   			HB		:A keep alive message.  If message is not received more then once a second then automatically disarmed.
Enable Arm 			- EA			EA		:Arms the launcher
Disable Arm			- DA			DA		:Disarms the launcher Launch channels are ignored  
Arm Status			- AS			ASx		:Checks the status of the Launcher "AS0" = Disarmed, "AS1" = Armed
Launch Channel 			- LCxx			LCxx		:Which channel to fire.  Ex. "LC01"
Channel Status 			- CSxx			CSx		:Ready = 0, Firing = 1, Open = 2 
Battery Voltage 		- BV			BVx.xx		:Battery Voltage


Bouns Items
Launch Time			- LTxxxx		LT		:How Long to turn on the igniter in ms.  
Error				- 			ERx		:Some sort of error number
